# About

This is my passion project I started over a while ago. This game is based in the universe of Forsaken, where you play as 007e7 and explore the neighborhood as you look for c00lkidd and cousin 007n7. With GASA4 gameplay, you collect endings to unlock the finale. I wanted a different ending to Forsaken, so I made it. It's kind of like a fangame or AU. 

After collecting all endings, you will unlock a cipher that you must decipher in order to open the **DOOR**. Behind the **DOOR** is the portal to the **Boss Fight**. You must fight to get them back. 

Make sure to stay after the Credits to see something cool...

I'm still working out the storyline and such, so everything here is not rock-solid.

# Note
Rojo does not integrate with Roblox Studio perfectly. I am unable to upload scripts under Tools or ServerStorage. 

Rojo only includes scripts under ServerScriptService, ReplicatedStorage, and StarterPlayerScripts by default.

I tried my best to move every script I could into those three services. 

Some scripts that must be instantiated/cloned under an object and require a reference to the Parent cannot be saved into Rojo. 

But you could always just play my game and reverse engineer it ;) you should totally play!!!
